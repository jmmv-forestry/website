--- title: "NetBSD 3.0 released" date: 2005-12-23 16:53:00 -0500 julipedia: 2005/12/netbsd-30-released.html slug: netbsd-30-released --- The NetBSD Project has just released a new major version of its operating system, thus making NetBSD 3.0. This release comes a year after of 2.0 publication and includes lots of new features and bug fixes. For more information, see the announcement (also available in Spanish).

From here, a big thank you to all the people who made this release possible and, specially, to the release engineering team.
